We rebel because we care, and because we can

What ZK is not…>
As the tagline goes, and unlike the iconic (albeit tragic), vintage James Dean movie on society’s moral decay that drove the lead character Stark to rebel, we have absolute faith in humanity. So, here are some house rules:

1. We DO NOT rebel against society.
We DO rebel against the hardships that crippled the society, such as the abominable contractual terms, or the unnecessary bureaucracies of the legal convention. We are simply, simplifying the process, without compromising the seriousness of the substance. We make friends, not foe. We genuinely care about who came through our doors, whether they ended up with us or not. 

2. We DO NOT do lip service.
The raison d'être behind ZK is service par excellence. Not the money. That should come, later (than sooner, or not, whatever). We do not promise the sun OR the moon. And we will not be coy. We are action figures, not sweet talkers. That has to be said, at the outset. 

3. We DO NOT tolerate arrogance.
From anybody. We ALL want good service. That is the fact. At ZK, good service starts with good people, and our people is our mainstay. We believe in easy, laidback co-working environment that will generate good vibes, nurture great ideas and deliver excellent results. So, we have no walls to divide us, no glass ceilings to limit us, no social class to ever judge us and no dress codes to typecast us. We are decent, in more ways than one and we owe it to no one to explain ourselves. WYSIWYG.
