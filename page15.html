La Famiglia

Mohd Zahir


Despite his serious no-nonsense attitude that he takes with him, he is also known to flabbergast the team with the silliest poses and college jokes. Busy as a bee, yet he never fails to spare a moment to share and pass on his 20 years of expertise and knowledge to fellow newcomers. The team captain is an excellent source to seek legal advice and opinions.

Khairul Khailani

He constantly reminds the team to “not mess with the best,” as he carries himself in stride. Arguably the funniest among the rest, he is extremely focused in handling his tasks successfully. A humor might brighten up this lad, but wait till he gets his hands on a microphone with a backing track running in the background. An excellent sensei in any legal matter. 

Nurzali

This gentleman is the newest partner as of April 2015. A jack of all civil litigation trades, he litigates and negotiates and dances his way around the Courtrooms. The Malaysian High Court is his playing ground, so is the badminton court. We are glad that he is on our side.
Many couldn’t comprehend how this busy litigator still has time for hobbies, he is a swell cook, he cycles at least 100 kilometers on weekends and he used to collect stamps, but with this digital era, he now collects selfie pictures with his Go Pro. 

Aifaa

Also a new member of the Bar, this lass has been through thick or thin with the family, mostly thick. Still eyeing on the elusive Malaysian Idol trophy, this songbird would have been a comfort to the firm with her singing between replying emails and drafting if only she could actually complete the whole song!!
A key person when it comes to commercial and corporate, you can hardly fail to notice her tears of joy when a six-inch corporate file is handed to her. Highly committed and well researched she is a valuable member of the team, without whom the firm will disintegrate.  

Ema

Our sassy prefect is a future advocate and solicitor in the making. With 10 years of experience in conveyancing, there isn’t a single file that will prevent her from a good night sleep after flossing. She understands the latest trends in fashion, though may need some help in containing her insatiable appetite to own new handbags. Nothing comes close between her and her files, she treats them like her own little children.
Not a single soul in the Family dares to cross her. Also fondly known as ‘Kak Long Kawasan’ 

Azreen

Our associate with 5 years of practice experience is a wide-eyed (both meanings applied) lady who specializes in conveyancing and real estate. She releases a very contagious laughter that extends unusually long that may cause worry among us, though she repeatedly assured us that she’s normal. No matter how lame your jokes are, you can always count on her laughter to make you feel good. She currently handles our projects and developer clients.   

Azza

She is the master Yoda of retail banking documentations. Very well versed in her area of expertise with 8 years of experience. Everything you need to know on loan documents is right on her fingertips. Mind you, she got nice, lovely fingers. The ladies in the team are envious of her slim figure, despite being a foodie. Hence, many think she has a darker secret that she’s not telling us (yet). 

Nana

We are professionals in handling legal matters, but questionable when it comes to numbers. That is when Nana comes in to help with our woes. Our very own Count von Count, she ensures our finances are kept in the black. You would find Aifaa and Ema particularly near to her desk close payday; since they still haven’t figured out how to cope with their urge to shop.  

Shah

Our youngest practitioner assists our associates in contracts. His enthusiasm promises himself a good future in the industry. He is currently assisting in litigation matters as well, he has brilliant research skills and has thirst for more knowledge. Along with Muzaqiam, he’s pretty much up there with the more grounded ones in the team.
Also known as the firm’s legal Wikipedia and someone who you can always depend on. 

Fazlin

Fazlin a.k.a Bai is the Hermes in the pantheon of ZK. Letters and documents will go through him, despite rain or shine, he will be on it without a grunt. The Inland Revenue, courts and Land authorities - he reigns. He is the only member everybody will wait for, including the Boss (if you want your documents to be on time).
